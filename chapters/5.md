This dungeon contains four new symbols: three stack operations (marked `:`, `;`, and `,` on the map) and one comparison (marked `=`). The stack operations have the following semantics:

- The `:` operation duplicates the number at the top of the stack.
- The `;` operation swaps the top two numbers of the stack.
- The `,` operation pops (and discards) the number at the top of the stack.

Whenever you step onto a `=` tile, you pop a number `b` from the stack, then pop another number `a` from the stack, and finally turn based on how the numbers compare. You turn 90 degrees left if `a < b`, or 90 degrees right if `a > b`. If the numbers are equal, your direction remains the same as before.

After you leave the dungeon, what number is at the top of the stack?
