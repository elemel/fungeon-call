This dungeon contains tiles with new symbols: digits (marked `0` through `9` on the map) and crossroads (marked `=`).

You pick up a ledger that someone left on the staircase. In the ledger, you maintain a stack of numbers. Adding a number onto the top of the stack is called pushing, while removing the top number of the stack is called popping. You start each dungeon exploration with an empty stack.

Whenever you walk onto a tile with digit, you push that digit onto the stack. Whenever you walk into a crossroads, you pop a number `b` from the stack, then pop another number `a` from the stack, and finally turn based on how the numbers compare. You turn 90 degrees left if `a` is less than `b`, or 90 degrees right if `a` is greater than `b`. You do not turn if the numbers are equal.

After how many ticks do you leave the dungeon?
